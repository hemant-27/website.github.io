Hemant Kumar

I am a 3rd year PhD student in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Maryland College Park, working with Prof. Nikhil Chopra at the SAS Lab. Previously, I was an undergraduate at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, where I obtained my bachelors degree and masters degree in Aerospace Engineering .

My research interests lie at the intersection of robotics, optimal decision-making, and generative AI. My recent works mostly focus on developing novel methods for contact-rich robotic manipulation tasks.
