Hemant Kumar

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Maryland College Park, working with Prof. Nikhil Chopra at the SAS Lab. Previously, I was an undergraduate at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, where I obtained a bachelors degree and masters degree in Aerospace Engineering .

I'm interested in Robot optimal decision making, Deep learning and Generative AI. My recent works mostly focus on developing novel methods for contact rich robot manipulation tasks.
